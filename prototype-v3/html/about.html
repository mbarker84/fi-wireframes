About us

Our mission is to serve the people shaping the web today and in the future: The designers, developers, content strategists, project managers, team leaders, CEOs and others working to make the web awesome. We do this by producing world-class conferences and workshops sharing the must-have skills and knowledge for building the web of tomorrow, as well as creating and curating quality content and facilitating connections. We want you to come with us on our journey.
